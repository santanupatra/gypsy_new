    Muze True Hi-Fi Bluetooth Speaker
    

                            
    
                                
    
                                    Enjoy high-quality music anywhere with the Muze True Hi-Fi Bluetooth Speaker. This gadget offers the convenience of portability without compromising the quality of sound.

    Enjoy high-quality music anywhere with the Muze True Hi-Fi Bluetooth Speaker. This gadget offers the convenience of portability without compromising the quality of sound. Muze produces sound that fills a whole room with wide staged surround sound. The clean crystal clear sound quality is complete with a deep bass, clear treble, and rich middle range. In addition, the sound quality doesn’t deteriorate when you crank up the volume. You certainly won’t get static hiss, insane vibrations or smoke coming out of the speakers either. It also has a frequency response range of 10Hz to 20 kHz. Wrapped in fabulous fabrics by Kvadrat, Muze features an elegant yet modern design. The speaker uses Bluetooth 4.2 connectivity for better speed and privacy. Finally, you can use Muze to wirelessly charge any Qi-enabled device by simply resting it on top.
